  • Cybeloras posted a comment Oct 18, 2012

    What OS do you play WoW in? OSX or Windows? Or Linux with Wine?

    The problem is, in case you were wondering, is that the files defined in the includes.xml files aren't being loaded from the correct paths. It gets to loading Conditions, and tries to open "Interface\AddOns\TellMeWhen\Components\Categories\includes.core.xml", but the correct location for this file is "Interface\AddOns\TellMeWhen\Components\Conditions\Categories\includes.core.xml". When it gets to this missing file, instead of skipping past it, the entire loading process halts immediately, leaving you with a mostly incomplete addon (thus the IconView.lua missing error)
