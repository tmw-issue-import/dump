    What steps will reproduce the problem?
    1. Set icon sorting priority by time (in any vertical group).
    2. Perform any action to pop out (display) some icons in a group (3 or more)
    3. Wait till icons start fading one by one

    What do you expect to happen? What happens instead?

    Expect - icon sorting by time, longest on the botom, shortest on the top. Group growing in the order specified by the settings (for example growing from the botom to the top)

    Happens - It either anchors on the top and grows botom with the shortest duration on the botom, or it anchors on the botom and grows top with the shortest duration on the top. The shortest duration is always in the direction of growth. Group grows without correct order. The top icon always stays on top, other icons can start growing bottom instead of the top. This leads to the gaps between icons etc.

    Also icon sorting by the "shown" condition not working at all.
    And this option for my opinion is quite important in many situations.
    For example boss cooldown tracker when you need to see the last used spell on the top of the list no mater the duration and icon id.

    Screenshot of the gaps: http://i.imgur.com/LaaJbb9.png

    Example with the icons:

    1. When all icons displayed at the same time (all seems alright):

    [60 cd]
    [30 cd]

    [5 cd ]

    2. Then icons start to fading (at 1st fading the 5 sec cd icon as example):

    [60 cd]
    GAP

    3. Then 15 sec cd icon expires:

    [60 cd]
    GAP

    [30 cd]

    Somehow the first icon stays at the top and the others growing to the bottom (instead of growing to the top).

  • Cybeloras posted a comment Dec 10, 2014

    Not broken - you have it configured wrong. Icons that are hidden can still have a duration, so your first sort priority should be Shown (respect/ignore Always Hide doesn't matter in this case), not Duration. Your second priority should then be Duration, and your third Icon ID.
