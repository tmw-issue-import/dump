    IMPORTANT: Attaching a saved variables file/screenshot will increase the likelihood of your problem being solved.
    Saved variables can be found in: "World of Warcraft/WTF/Account/<Account Name>/SavedVariables/TellMeWhen. lua"

    EDIT:
    I just found the cause of this issue, I had added "unit level (player)" > 80 as condition to the icon of Inquisition. Apparently something goes wrong when this is being checked. It worked fine before 4.1 however.

    What steps will reproduce the problem?
    On my paladin, if I'm in Retribution spec, the icons of TMW don't show. There is one group that I use for all specs, however it only displays properly in Protection spec, not in Ret. The paladin in question is Orozar on Saurfang. On my other character (Pent on Saurfang) the icons display just fine on both specs.

    What do you expect to happen? What happens instead?
    I expect the icons to show when I'm missing certain buffs, such as Seal of Truth or Blessing of Might. Other icons which also aren't working show the buffs Zealotry and Avenging Wrath, and Inquisition with only a couple seconds left.

    What version of TellMeWhen are you using? ("The latest" is not a version)
    4.1.1
