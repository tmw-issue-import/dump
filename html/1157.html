  • Ayiu posted a comment Sep 14, 2015

    I don't want to track the rogue using Shroud of Concealment, I want to track the buff that happens on the opposing player with shroud of concealment.

    If an aura event is created, it'll show where the source of Shroud came from, and who it was applied to.

    if not auralogger then auralogger=CreateFrame("Frame")

    end;auralogger:RegisterEvent

    ("COMBAT_LOG_EVENT_UNFILTERED");auralogger:SetScript

    ("OnEvent",function

    (_,_,timestamp,event,_,sourceGUID,sourceName,sourceFlags,sourceR

    aidFlags,destGUID,destName,destFlags,destRaidFlags,spellID,spell

    Name)if string.find(event,"AURA") then print(event..": aura:

    \""..tostring(spellName).."\"; source: \""..tostring

    (sourceName).."\"; destination: \""..tostring(destName).."\"")

    end end)

    Will show both from the stealth target and the destination target. I just specifically want an icon to show on the target that was buffed with Shroud, not the target that created it.

    e.g. rogue(arena1) uses shroud while stealth, shaman(arena2)receives visible buff(Shroud of Concealment)for a split second before entering stealth, I just want an icon that stays there and continues to count down from 15s, and will go away if the shaman comes out and enters combat. ONLY on the shaman, not the rogue that is still in stealth.
