    It not showing up in the interface menu is a bug that i am aware of, here is one way to work around it (hopefully it will work): Type the following:

    /run TellMeWhen_Settings.DSN = {} ReloadUI()

    (Note that this will reload your UI, so don't do it in the middle of a bossfight or something)
